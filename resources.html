Further Reading

Criptiques (2014, edited by Caitlin Wood)

An anthology with pieces written by writers and activists with disabilities.

Black Disabled Art History 101 (2017, by Leroy F. Moore, Jr.)

A children's book that highlights the works of Black artists with disabilities.

Disability Visibility: First-person stories from the twenty-first century (2020, edited by Alice Wong)

A collection of contemporary essays by writers with disabilities.

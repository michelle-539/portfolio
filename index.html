Disability Rights Activism

My name’s Michelle, and I’m a master’s student at the School of Information. Ever since taking a Disability Studies course in undergrad, I’ve been interested in this topic. Naturally, then, I chose to create a website about disability rights activism!

This website will focus on activism within the United States from date to date. I know there’s much going on in this space around the world. However, I didn’t want to have a focus so broad that I lost the nuances of each country’s history of activism.

As a disclaimer, I’ve done my best to make this website as accessible as I can by using accessibility validators and following best practices where possible. If you notice anything that could be improved, please let me know by filling out this contact form.
